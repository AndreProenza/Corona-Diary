Spain has 399 more deaths and more than 200,000 infected by Covid-19

Fidelity: High

Spain has registered, in the last 24 hours, 399 deaths due to the new coronavirus, a decrease compared to the previous day, with a total of 20,852 fatalities in the country so far.

According to the Spanish Ministry of Health, there are 4,266 newly infected - a slight increase compared to Sunday (2.2%) -, which brings the total number of people who contracted the disease to 200,210.

Since the start of the new coronavirus pandemic, 80,587 people have been considered cured.

The region with the most positive Covid-19 cases is Madrid, with 56,963 infected and 7,351 dead, followed by Catalonia (41,676 and 4,009), Castile-La Mancha (16,796 and 2,021), Castile and León (15,857 and 1,493) and Basque Country (12,628 and 1,081).

Source : Sic Noticias - 20/04/2020


Spain prolongs state of emergency but alleviates child confinement

Fidelity: High

The Spanish government wants to extend the state of emergency until May 9, but minors will be able to leave their homes before that date. Children's measurements and age are not yet defined.

Pedro Sánchez, President of the Government of Spain, will transmit to the presidents of the autonomous communities the Government's proposal to extend the state of emergency for another 15 days, until the 9th of May. But there are measures of social confinement that will be alleviated, especially with regard to minors, whose reality may change as of April 27th.

The reasons that can justify the departure of the home by minors, as well as the security measures to be adopted, have not yet been defined. It is also not clear at what age the minors will be able to go out into the street, but the reference point, writes El País, it is the Public Health Law (which provides for children up to 12 years old).

Source : Observador - 18/04/2020
