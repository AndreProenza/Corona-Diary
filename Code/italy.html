Italy exceeds 25,000 dead

Fidelity: High

In the last 24 hours, Italy registered 437 deaths and 3,370 new cases of coronaviruses. The number of fatalities increased from 24,648 to 25,085 compared to yesterday. Since the beginning of the pandemic, 187,327 people have been infected in the country.

The number of recoveries rose from 51,600 to 54,543.

There are 2,384 people in intensive care, 87 fewer than on Tuesday.

Source : Sic Noticias - 22/04/2020


Italian Federation admits finishing championship only in November

Fidelity: High

The Italian Football Federation (FIGC) reinforced this Wednesday that its "absolute priority" is finish the current season of Serie A and pointed to November as a possibility, being available for delay the start of the 2020/21 season.

"Our absolute priority is to finish the exams. There are no deadlines. If we can play in June, we will. If necessary, we resumed in September and ended the championship in November, with the next season starting in January of next year," said the Federation president Gabriele Gravina, speaking to the Italian newspaper La Repubblica.
